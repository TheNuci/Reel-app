    Privacy Policy

    Last updated July 2026

    1. Who we are

    Reelicious is the data controller for personal data processed in the app. We're based in the EU and follow the GDPR.

    2. What we collect

    Account data (email), the links and recipes you import, recipes you save and share, photos you upload, and basic usage analytics to improve the app.

    3. How we use it

    To provide the service (generating and storing your recipes), to process your subscription, and to improve features. Recipe generation uses automated AI processing of the video's audio and captions.

    4. Sharing

    We don't sell your data. We use processors (hosting, payments, AI generation) who act on our instructions under data-processing agreements.

    5. Your rights

    You can access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time from Settings, or by contacting us. Deleting your account removes your recipes and uploaded photos.
